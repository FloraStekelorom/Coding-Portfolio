About

Flora Hewitt

I started my career in Finance and worked in Mergers and Acquisitions for more than 7 years in various different banks and consulting firms. I then decided to get into technology, took a range of different courses and have specialised in web design, web development and user experience. In several years, I have managed to build a multiple of different websites from portfolios, e-commerce websites to networking platforms.

I engage in freelancing work for various different industries. I tend to work remotely but I keep in touch with my clients regularly to make sure that the end product is exactly what they wished for.
